Reflections

2017
Solist

A short work for solo instrumentalist. Composed in collaboration with a simple algorithm, Reflections falls in a unique position between structured improvisation, "standard" (deterministic) music, and stochastic processes. Any instrumentalist with an interest in performing the piece should fill out the table below and generate a new, unique score fitted to their instrument. In an ideal setting, a new score should be used for each performance, but due to the technical demands of the piece, it is understood that this is not always feasible.
