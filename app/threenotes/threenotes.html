Three Notes

2018
Solist

A musical offering. Left intentionally open, musical material is to be filled by the performing forces. Type of sustaining motion, dynamics, shaping, instrumentation, and all other musical decisions are left open to showcase the performing forces and their unique musical abilities. However, each pitch should appear only in the specified octave. Total duration is left up to the discretion of concert programming and can be altered to fit desired lengths. Suggested durations are be between two minutes and half an hour. Because every performance of a work is always unique, a fresh score should be used for every occasion.
