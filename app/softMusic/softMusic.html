Soft Music

2019
Ensemble of Voices

About the work:
Soft music is an exploration of structure and construction out of some of the smallest and most incidental parts of every musical experience: breath. This work is generative and open instrumentation, and is for any number of voices that one might happen to have in an ensemble.

To Perform:
Each performer will play their audio part through earbuds or headphones off of their own cell phone so only they can hear it. This audio part contains instructions of what the performer is to do. The organizer of the performance should distribute a 'seed' to every member of the ensemble ("helloworld", "asdf", or something), as well as a part number (1,2, etc.). The performers then can generate a copy of the score on their phones and select which part they will be performing. When it is time to perform the work, every performer will hit "play" on their audio at the same time. They should then follow the instructions played out to them, and the work concludes when everyone is told the piece is over.
