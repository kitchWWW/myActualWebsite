My Battery Is Low
And It Is Getting Dark

2019
Solo Keyboard Percussion and Live Electronics


This work was commissioned as part of the Electronic Integration Project by Jordan Walsh. The title of this piece comes from the infamous last words of Mars rover Opportunity, who sent the message on June 10, 2018 as a dust storm was approaching.

The piece is currently under the exclusive access of members of the Electronic Integration Project (EIP), and will be so until 2021. If you would like to perform the work, please reach out to Jordan Walsh for the opportunity to join the commissioning consortium.

Due to the nature of the EIP, new scores can be generated freely below, but the electronics are available only by request to members of the consortium.
