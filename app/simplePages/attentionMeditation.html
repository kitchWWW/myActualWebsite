Attention Meditation

2024
Open Instrumentation


This music, it doesn’t groove –
It’s like cars driving past on the freeway.

Performed by The Arm Trio
Jason Charney, guitar/electronics
Sarah Manley, trombone/electronics
Patrick McMinn, trumpet/electronics

These meditations aim to create soundscapes that draw attention to their various components, gradually emphasizing and minimizing different voices in the overall sonic mixture by modifying different sonic characteristics. This creates a musical field that encourages the listener to engage with the multiplicity of sound, and all of the different ways performers can manifest sound into the world.

Performed at Peabody's Goodwin Recital Hall
