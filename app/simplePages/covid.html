Obligatory COVID-19 Art

2020
Performance Art


As an active composer alive in March 2020, this is my obligatory submission of an artistic work in a web-based format for distribution to express my personal declamation on the state of global and personal affairs in light of the current situation.

Notes on performance:
  • Performer is tied to the guitar such that their slight movements cause larger-than-life (sonic) effects.
  • Performer will be generally unable to move from the immediate area as they execute (dance) actions that are typically mundane.
  • The backdrop of the performance should be one over-saturated with a single (visual) theme.
  • Should the performer's bindings fall off of during the performance, the performer is to frantically put the twine bindings back on, as it is imperative for saving (musical) lives.
  • stay home

For solo performer, electric guitar, loose binding (twine), PJs, live electronics, and an empty apartment.
