Song Cycle

2017
Duet - Violin and Typist

Song Cycle is a work in three movements, setting to music the poetry of Riley Welch. For violin and typist, the performance makes use of ChucK, a software for live music performance. Performance instructions and program notes can be found in the score, avaliable here. The work was premiered by Kathryn Drake and Kendall Floyd on May 15th, 2017 in Austin.
