AGENDA(S) Project

2020
collaborative improvisation


A live group improvisation on the idea of separate agendas, and how each agenda influences, interrupts, or compliments the other. Performed March 8th at the University of Texas at Austin, as part of S.E.E.D. (Students Exhibiting Experimental Dance)

Dance - Atticus Griffin, Avery Moore
Projection - Michael Bruner
Sound - Brian Ellis
