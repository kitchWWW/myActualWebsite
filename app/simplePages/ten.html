Ten Water Events

2023
dancer and live electronics


Link to the web-based instrument for performers.

Ten Water Events is an unadorned exploration of water, expression, and release. Featuring the collected sounds of waterfalls from across the continent, the work highlights our relationship to our our bodies, to light, to rain, and to warm sunny Sunday afternoons.

Brian Ellis, Sound Design, Creative Coding
Erin Landers, Choreography
Sophie Delphis, Dancer

Premiered by The Brooklyn Motion Capture Dance Ensemble
