purplegreen is a meditation on translation. This work invites the audience to contemplate the transformation and reinterpretation of sounds as they journey through different mediums and processes, as well as varied modes of listening as they are tasked with deciding the relative levels of attention to give the competing versions of the same material.

The work is available as both a website installation and a live performance piece.
