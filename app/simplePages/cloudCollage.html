Cloud Collage

2023
cellphone orchestra


Excerpt from Discarded Clouds, a live evening of theatre directed by Tianding He. Premiered at HERE Arts Center on December 15th, 2023.

To perform this work, a facilitator should load the control panel on a computer and display the QR code to the audience. After everyone has joined, and the sounds of bells have filled the air, the facilitator should begin the automated playback of the piece.
