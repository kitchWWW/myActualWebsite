To Make A Short Story Long

2021
youtube DJ improvisation


Want to try this yourself? (or use your own youtube videos?) Click here!

A live youtube DJ improvisation with YouTuber, an online web tool designed for appropriating youtube playback as a time stretching video sampling utility. This particular improvisation utilizes two recordings from SANS; duo, both performing from a single score, provided below. These recordings (as well as their reversals) were then uploaded to youtube to be utilized in this mixing setup, allowing for a single performer to manipulate and perform with the sounds of the collective ensemble.
