These Special Hands

2024
For Soloist and Audience Cellphone Orchestra


There is the old quote that goes “time slips through our fingers like grains of sand… the more you squeeze, the more sand trickles through your fingers” Music is not a tactile thing. As a time based medium, you and I can not hold on to the music that surrounds us any more than we can hold onto water in the ocean. However, with this piece, I try to give you special hands. Hands that can, for a moment, pause time and keep it there. Hands that can hold the music you hear closer to you.

Originally created in 2022, score and electronics revised in 2024.
