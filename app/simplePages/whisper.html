Whisper Garden

2021
portable installation

bury in the earth
only those things
that can not be voiced

so that if they may survive
being packed into the dirt
squished
shunned
ostracized
and replayed endlessly over sleepless nights

they may eventually blossom
and see the light of day
into your whisper garden

Installation consisting of flowers, vases, projection art, computer, speakers, and playtronica. Powered via Chrome, WebMIDI, and pizzicato.js. Available online here. Projection art, sound design, and installation by Brian Ellis.
