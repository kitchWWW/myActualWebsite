zinetude

2024
flexible ensemble of 2 or more


zinetude is a 7 minute choose-your-own-adventure soundscape for you to create with your friends!

Print out as many sheets from the PDF as you'll have performers (plus maybe a few extra) and then fold each sheet into a zine - instructions here. Each page is different, so feel free to use different books for each performance, or to swap books if rehearsing it multiple times.

Score if your group is 2-4 people
