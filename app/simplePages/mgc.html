Margins Guitar Collective

2021
solo recital


An evening length program of new music for the classical guitar and live electronics! This program featured four world premiers as well as the presentation of several projects several years in the making. Classic 70s Minimalism, SANS;duo, Live Electronics, and reappropriation of social media tools all make an appearance! Program originally presented by the Margins Guitar Collective.

Program:

Change   -   Meredith Monk
Joy Boy   -   Julius Eastman
In Any Random Shape Or form   -   Samara Rice
To Make a Short Story Long   -   Brian Ellis
La Guitarra   -   Max Addae
Circular Canon   -   Ryann Daisy Swimmer
Cenas Remotas   -   Rodrigo Baggio
