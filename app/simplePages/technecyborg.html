Techne Cyborg

2022
concert series


Techne Cyborg was a two night concert series and art gallery showing at pinkFROG cafe in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, showcasing musical and visual works that engage in human-computer interaction. The first night's program was intense and thought provoking contemporary classical music, "The Cyborg Thinks". The second night consisted of three ambient sets, and was titled "The Cyborg Dreams". Additionally, a corresponding art installation was presented consisting of paintings, generative photographs, data bending, and wire sculpture.
