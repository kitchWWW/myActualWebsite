Sound; Machine; Sound; Me; Sound

2024
Installation


Drawing from Pauline Oliveros' practice of Deep Listening, and Alvin Lucier "I am sitting in a room", this installation invites mechanized listeners into the practice of sound art co-creation along with human practitioners. In this installation, Humans, along with machines, are invited to:

Listen for a sound
Think about the sound
Produce the sound from your thoughts about the sound
Repeat

Humans and computers both follow these instructions, humans via their intuitive relationship to sound and language, and computers via an assortment of AI models. For the computers, sound from the space is recorded by a microphone, which is then described using an audio-to-text AI model (whisper derived). These textual descriptions are then fed to a text-to-audio AI model (AudioGen) which generates a sound file, which is then played back live in the room.
