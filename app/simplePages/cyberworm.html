Cyberworm

2023
AR Puppet


The cyberworm is an augmented reality puppet designed and built for the show How I Disappeared lead by Tianding He. The puppet consists of a camera, laptop, and projector that takes the actor's movements and uses them to animate (in real time) the motions of an animation on the screen. In this way, Non Kuramoto (the puppeteer) can control the digital cyberworm via motion capture with their movements.

This version of the cyberworm was premiered at the New Ohio Theater on August 2nd, 2023.
