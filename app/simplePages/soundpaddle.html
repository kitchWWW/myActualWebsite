Gowanus Sound Paddle

2024
a sound walk, but with canoes

Listen online here!



A sound walk that introduces paddlers to the history behind the waterway, the ecology, industrialization that lead to the canal's pollution, and the cleanup efforts that have emerged in recent years.

Composed by Brian Ellis
Narration by Jos Prol
Script by Ford Fourqurean and Jos Prol

Recordings by Unheard-of Ensemble members
Ford Fourqurean, clarinet, Daniel Anastasio, piano
