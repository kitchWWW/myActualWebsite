Fleet

2018
Composition / Collaboration

Part of the Ears, Eyes + Feet collaborative concert series at the University of Texas at Austin.

Fleet is a work that explores the depth of expression that can be created and explored within a limited set of simple actions, sounds, and forms. With nautical implications and glacial pacing, this dynamic expression on full display is no more and no less than what you experience in our fleeting moment together.



Audio composed in Max/MSP.
