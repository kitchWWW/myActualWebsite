Epoch Music

2016
Small Ensemble

Vivaldi was fond of saying that he could write concertos faster than his copyist could make the parts. Inspired by this, Epoch Music is a program that generates a multi-movement work which loosely follows the form of a Vivaldi concerto. The work can be for any sized ensemble, performing in any tonality, and, by utilizing constrained randomness, generates the fresh work for the ensemble.
