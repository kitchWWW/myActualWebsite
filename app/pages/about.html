Brian Ellis.

Brian Ellis is an Austin based composer, researcher, and guitarist. He is currently enrolled at the University of Texas at Austin where he is a Computer Science Honors and Music double major. His musical influences include many artists from different areas including Philip Glass, The Show Ponies, Mauro Giuliani, David Cope's Emmy, and John Williams - primarily the guitarist but on occasion the film score composer as well.

As a Computer Scientist, Brian has interest in artificial intelligence and algorithms for genomics, big data, and machine learning. He works in Howard Ochman's lab on this project, and as a code monkey makes chrome apps and tells computers how to spit out music. Work can be viewed on gitHub and more information found on his work website or resume.
